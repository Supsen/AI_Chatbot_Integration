Function: Personal Loan & Mortgage Calculator

This function will calculate the monthly payment for a loan based on:
	•	Loan Amount ($)
	•	Annual Interest Rate (%)
	•	Loan Term (Years)

Formula Used:

M = \frac{P \times r}{1 - (1 + r)^{-n}}

Where:
	•	M = Monthly Payment
	•	P = Loan Amount
	•	r = Monthly Interest Rate (Annual Rate / 12)
	•	n = Total Number of Payments (Years × 12)
